Common Law Aptitude Test

The Common Law Admission Test (CLAT) is a national level entrance exam for admissions to undergraduate (UG)
and postgraduate (PG) law programmes offered by 22 National Law Universities around the country.

Eligibility:

1. There will be no upper age limit for UG Programme in CLAT 2023.

2. Minimum percentage of marks in 10+2 or an equivalent examination

  • Forty-five percent (45%) marks or equivalent grade of candidates belonging to General / OBC / PWD
    / NRI / PIO / OCI
  • Forty Percent (40%) marks or equivalent in case of candidates belonging to SC/ST categories.

CLAT Pattern

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Mode:Offline
  • Total Questions: 120
